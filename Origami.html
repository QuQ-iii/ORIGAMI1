VIDEO TUTORIAL FOR SNAKE :


Did you know that out of the many species of snakes, only the female King Cobra snakes build nests! They do it only when they are close to laying eggs. It is very interesting to observe how a limbless animal like this one can build a nest

Other facts about snakes include :

  1. Snakes are carnivores (meat eaters).
  2. Snakes don't have eyelids.
  3. Snakes can't bite food so have to swallow it whole.
  4. Snakes have flexible jaws which allow them to eat prey bigger than their head!
  5. Snakes are found on every continent of the world except Antarctica.
  6. Snakes have internal ears but not external ones.
